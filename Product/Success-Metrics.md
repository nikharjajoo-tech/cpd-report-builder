# Success metrics framework

This is the KPI framework defined before launch. For the actual results achieved against it, see [Case-Study/Metrics.md](../Case-Study/Metrics.md).

## Business metrics
- First-draft turnaround time
- Number of concurrent engagements the firm can support
- SME hours spent per report on drafting versus validation

## Product metrics
- Chapters generated per engagement
- Reviewer edit rate per AI-generated chapter
- Time from ingestion to first full draft
- Adoption of AI-assisted editing versus manual authoring

## AI metrics
- Citation accuracy
- Validation agent flag rate
- Retrieval precision across the engagement corpus
- Prompt benchmark score against gold-standard sections

## Operational metrics
- Document ingestion success rate
- Ingestion throughput at the 300MB threshold
- SharePoint sync reliability
- Version history and reviewer workflow completion rate

## Why four categories, not one dashboard
Business metrics justify the platform to firm leadership. Product metrics show whether consultants actually adopted AI-assisted drafting instead of quietly working around it. AI metrics are what a reviewer needs to trust the output, and operational metrics are what keeps ingestion, the foundation everything else depends on, reliable at scale.
